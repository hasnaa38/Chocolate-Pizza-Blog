      For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have a chumky-jammy mixtire. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and ler cool completely.

      Ice cream: In a small pot ever medium heat, combine milk, and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Ser mixture in the fridge and wait until the mixture cooled.

      Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacturer instructions, i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes hefore adding mascarpone, fig jam mixrure, and the nuts. Continue to precess for +/-10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyres dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for least two hours before serving.

  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup mascarpone
  • 1/2 tsp pink salt
  • 1 lb Black Hission Figs
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2-4 tbsp water
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/3 granutated sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 lemon, Juiced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup honey roasted pecans, roughly chopped
